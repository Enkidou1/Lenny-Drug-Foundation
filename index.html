About Us

Our Mission

We, at Lenny Advocate against Drug Abuse Foundation (LAADAF), join forces with government agencies and organizations to educate and eradicate drug abuse among young individuals. We passionately raise awareness, establish drug-free clubs, and actively engage through media and seminars to combat the harmful effects of drug abuse..

Our Approach

Through interactive programs and evidence-based education, we provide the tools needed to resist peer pressure and avoid substance abuse.

Our Impact

We've reached over 100,000 students across the country, significantly reducing drug use rates in participating schools.

Our Programs

School Workshops

Interactive sessions designed to engage students and provide them with practical knowledge about drug abuse prevention.

Peer Mentoring

Training programs that empower students to become positive influences within their social circles.

Family Support

Resources and counseling services for families affected by drug abuse, promoting healing and recovery.
